Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 28, 2024

Summary: Heart Rate Monitor does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All measurements are stored locally on your device only.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Heart Rate Monitor ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect or transmit any personal information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Heart Rate Monitor does not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Health data to external servers
  • Any data transmitted over the internet

Camera Permission

The app requires camera permission solely to measure your heart rate using photoplethysmography (PPG) technology. The camera is used to:

  • Detect light variations through your fingertip
  • Calculate your heart rate based on blood flow patterns

No images or videos are captured, stored, or transmitted. The camera data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded after calculating your heart rate.

Local Data Storage

Your heart rate measurements are stored locally on your device in an internal database. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to other apps
  • Is not backed up to cloud services by the app
  • Can be deleted at any time using the "Clear All" feature in the app

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks. There are no SDKs that collect or process your data.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is intended for general wellness purposes for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

  • Device encryption
  • Screen lock protection
  • App sandboxing

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your measurements in the History screen
  • Delete: Remove individual measurements or clear all history
  • Portability: Your data stays on your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Medical Disclaimer

Heart Rate Monitor is intended for wellness and fitness purposes only. It is not a medical device and should not be used for medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
